## Problem (from description)

- Uses a different GAN architecture (not BigGAN/StyleGAN as in Hu et al.) applied against YOLO for naturalistic adversarial patch generation.
- Provides a comparison point for different generative model choices in patch naturalism.

## Why This Matters

Provides a data point for the question: does the choice of GAN architecture significantly affect adversarial effectiveness? Comparing AYO-GAN's architecture to BigGAN (Hu et al.) and the diffusion model approach (Diff-NAT) gives a methodological comparison for the capstone's related work section.
